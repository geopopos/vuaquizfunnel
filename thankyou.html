Industry Jargon, Simplified

Confused about industry terms? Here's a quick guide:

  • Lead: Basic contact details from potential clients.
  • Appointment: A pre-set meeting time, which might not always materialize.
  • Inbound Call: Direct calls, sometimes from non-genuine sources.
  • Live Transfer: Our forte. We nurture the lead and connect you live, ensuring genuine interest and a higher chance of conversion.
